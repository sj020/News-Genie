# News-Genie

The advent of the internet has boosted the information consumption by users. Since the production of information has become easier, 
the rate of information flow has grown tremendously. This trend has made the selection of information difficult for the user.
There are thousands of news publications that produce millions of news articles in a single day and to gain the attention of the reader 
some publishers generate catchy headlines but sometimes these headlines could be misleading. Those misleading headlines can give false 
sense of security to the reader regarding the whole content of the article which is dangerous in the long run. As Alexander Pope said that “Little Knowledge is a Dangerous thing” we are trying to solve the problem using Deep learning.

News Genie is an app which will generate meaningful headlines and summaries from the news. The Goal is to generate a headlines & summary which is more relevant to the story and capture the meaning of the story being published.

News Genie is using Transformers like BART, T5 and BERT which are finetuned on Economic Times news data.
